Some Facts About Camels

  1. They can survive without water for several weeks
  2. They have long eyelashes which protect their eyes from dus storms
  3. they have padded feet so they dont sink in the sand

Some Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • They are reowned for their outstanding navigational skills
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Some Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  • Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism
  • Their lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators

Some Facts About Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day

Some Facts About Teddy Bear

  1. Their origin story doesn’t have a happy ending
  2. Teddy bears mourned the Titanic
  3. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902

Some Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues
